Frequently Asked Questions

Is Wallpath free to play?

Yes! Wallpath is free to download and play. The game offers optional in-app purchases for additional content and features.

How many levels are available?

Wallpath currently features over 100 handcrafted puzzles across multiple difficulty levels, with new content added regularly.

Do I need internet to play?

No, Wallpath can be played completely offline. However, an internet connection is required for daily challenges and updates.

Is Wallpath available on Android?

Currently, Wallpath is only available on iOS devices. We're considering an Android version in the future based on player interest.
